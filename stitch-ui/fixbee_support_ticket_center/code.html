Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a screen repair take? expand_more
Most screen repairs are completed within 90 minutes if you visit a BeeHub. For doorstep pickup, the average turnaround time is 24-48 hours including transit.
Is my data safe during repair? expand_more
We prioritize your privacy. All technicians follow a strict no-access data protocol. However, we always recommend a full backup before any hardware intervention.
What does the FixBee warranty cover? expand_more
Our standard 1-year warranty covers all parts and labor related to the specific repair performed. Physical or liquid damage after repair is not covered.
