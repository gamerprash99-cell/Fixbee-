Tagline

---

Product Overview

FixBee is a premium device-care platform that helps users repair smartphones, purchase accessories, track repairs in real time, manage device history, and protect their devices through a single trusted ecosystem.

FixBee aims to become India's most trusted platform for device ownership and maintenance.

---

Problem Statement

Millions of smartphone users face challenges when repairing and maintaining their devices.

Common problems include:

• Untrusted local repair shops

• Unclear pricing

• Fake spare parts

• No repair tracking

• No centralized repair history

• Difficulty finding compatible accessories

• No digital device records

Users spend thousands on smartphones but have no trusted platform to manage the complete lifecycle of their devices.

---

Solution

FixBee provides a complete device-care ecosystem where users can:

• Book trusted repairs

• Track repair progress live

• Buy accessories

• Store device information

• Maintain repair history

• Purchase protection plans

• Get customer support

All from one mobile application.

---

Core Value Proposition

FixBee combines:

• Device Repair

• Accessories Shopping

• Repair Tracking

• Device Vault

• Protection Plans

• Support Services

into a single platform.

Unlike traditional repair shops or online stores, FixBee manages the entire device ownership journey.

User Stories

Customer

As a customer, I want to book a repair so that I can fix my device easily.

As a customer, I want to track repairs so that I know the status of my device.

As a customer, I want to save my devices so that I can manage warranties and service history.

As a customer, I want to buy compatible accessories so that I avoid incorrect purchases.

As a customer, I want support so that I can resolve issues quickly.

---

Technician

As a technician, I want to receive assigned jobs so that I can manage my workload.

As a technician, I want to upload proof and updates so that customers can trust the repair process.

---

Admin

As an admin, I want to manage users, products, orders, and technicians so that I can operate the platform efficiently.
